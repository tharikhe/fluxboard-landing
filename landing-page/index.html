Why Choose fluxBoard?

Llama 3.3 Intelligence

Rewrite, grammar-correct, summarize, or translate your text dynamically in real time without exiting any chat application.

Predictive Auto-Suggest

Gboard-style context-aware suggestions, smart contraction correction (e.g., dont to don't), and clean space-aligned punctuation inputs.

Long-Press Shortcut Keys

Long-press numbers on the top row to type without switching menus, or long-press spacebar to switch keyboards instantly.

Smooth Emoji Board

Beautiful, category-sorted layout (Smileys, Gestures, Hearts) that lets you scroll through hundreds of emojis instantly and smoothly.

How To Install & Setup

1

Download & Open the APK

Tap the download button above on your Android device to download app-release.apk. Open it to begin installation.

2

Allow Installation from Unknown Sources

If prompted by your browser or system, toggle on "Allow installation from this source". fluxBoard is secure and requires no special system permissions.

3

Follow the Interactive Guide

Launch the fluxBoard app. Tap the onscreen buttons to easily Enable fluxBoard in settings and select it as your Default Input Method.
